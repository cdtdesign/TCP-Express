Welcome to the Traveling Children Project!
Your passport to fun experiences! Join the journey and collect Travel Tokens that will reward you with fun prizes and great discounts to awesome destinations.

After sign up, here’s how to join the journey… online and offline
ONLINE
1. Download the Journey Kit.
Click Download to get the Journey Kit and construct.
2. Use the Destination Search.
Find information about destinations you want to visit.
3. Visit a fun new destination.
Journey to destination, have fun and snap a pic with TC.
4. Create post at the Journey Blog.
Receive Travel Tokens when sharing your passport at the Journey Blog.
OFFLINE
1. Printout Journey Kit.
Cut and color TC, then construct compass and passport.
2. Use Journey Compass.
Find next adventure using compass for journey ideas.
3. Snap pics with TC.
Journey to destination and say “cheese” with Travel Guide, TC.
4. Write in Passport Journal.
Later, reflect and record your experience in passport.
It’s not JUST about the destination, but also enjoying the journey along the way!
