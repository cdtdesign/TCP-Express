Travel Token Reward Program

Venues can become Official Traveling Children Project Destination Token Sponsors when choosing from the following levels of sponsorship by offering discounted admissions as rewards to our Travelers who earn Travel Tokens through posting their journeys at the Journey Blog. Travelers will be able to redeem their Travel Tokens as they are rewarded or they may allow them to accumulate. When a Traveler proceeds to redeem their tokens for rewards, they will have access to 1 destination discount from each of the 4 levels of sponsorship. This equates to 3 Travel Tokens per discount reward.

LEVELS OF SPONSORSHIP —
Token Sponsors can choose from varying levels of sponsorship that would include:

Yellow Token Sponsorship Package: 25% discounted admissions
EXPOSURE: 1) Inclusion of sponsor logo on website Sponsorship Panel; 2) Sponsor logo printed in Passport Journal or backside of Traveling Christian

Green Token Sponsorship Package: 50% discounted admissions
EXPOSURE: 1) Inclusion of sponsor logo on website Sponsorship Panel; 2) Sponsor logo printed in Passport Journal or backside of Traveling Christian; 3) Sponsor ad on Journey Blog page @ 200x200; 4) Sponsor ad has click-able outbound link

Purple Token Sponsorship Package: 75% discounted admissions
EXPOSURE: 1) Inclusion of sponsor logo on website Sponsorship Panel; 2) Sponsor logo printed in Passport Journal or backside of Traveling Christian; 3) Sponsor ad on Journey Blog page @ 450x450; 4) Sponsor ad on Destination Search page @ 450x450; 5) Sponsor ads have click-able outbound links; 6) 1/4 page, full-color ad on flier included in Journey Kit

Blue Token Sponsorship Package: FREE admissions
EXPOSURE: 1) Inclusion of sponsor logo on website Sponsorship Panel; 2) Sponsor logo printed in Passport Journal or backside of Traveling Christian; 3) Sponsor ad on Journey Blog page @ 450x450; 4) Large banner sponsor ad on Destination Search page @ 900x400; 5) Sponsor ads have click-able outbound links; 6) Full page, full-color flier ad included in Journey Kit

Explore the World Program

What is the Traveling Children Project – Explore the World Program?

Unfortunately in this world not every child is equally exposed to the same level of opportunities. At Traveling Children Program we want to change this reality… one child at a time. The Explore the World Program is a grant based program that allows children from at-risk communities and schools to have opportunities to take advantage of these wonderful learning adventures and aspire beyond their circumstance.

Parents, guardians, mentors and organizations can submit applications on behalf of a single underprivileged child, up to a large group of underprivileged children for consideration of sponsorship through this program. Application would collect the financial data to qualify each child, while also inquiring on the 12 destinations each child would like to explore–up to 2 destinations from each destination category.
